Cartier eyewear

Founded in Paris in 1847, Cartier brings its legendary craftsmanship from fine jewelry and watches into the world of luxury eyewear. At Eye Atelier in Old Bridge, NJ, we curate a refined selection of Cartier prescription eyeglasses and sunglasses that reflect the maison's signature design codes.

We don't stock everything — we carry only the pieces we believe in. Our clients come from Old Bridge, Marlboro, Manalapan, Matawan, East Brunswick, Morganville, Holmdel and throughout Middlesex and Monmouth Counties seeking a thoughtful, unhurried fitting rather than a quick transaction.

The collections we curate

Cartier organizes its eyewear into iconic collection families inspired by its most celebrated watches and jewelry. Our selection rotates frequently and typically draws from:

  • Première de Cartier — The pinnacle of the line. 18k gold and platinum finishes, signature vis screws, and luxurious acetate or buffalo horn temples. Unmistakably Cartier.
  • Santos de Cartier — Inspired by the 1904 Santos pilot watch. Structured rectangular silhouettes with exposed screws — architectural, masculine, and timeless.
  • Panthère de Cartier — Featuring the iconic panther motif. Soft cat-eye and rounded shapes with feline elegance — quietly bold.
  • Pasha de Cartier — Generous round silhouettes with confident proportions and clean lines.
  • Trinity de Cartier — The celebrated three-band gold motif (yellow, white, and rose) elegantly worked into the temples.
  • Signature C de Cartier & Décor C — The distinctive C monogram integrated into temples and bridges. Everyday luxury without heavy gold.

We're happy to help you match your Cartier eyewear to your existing Love Bracelet, Juste un Clou, or other signature pieces.

Materials and finish

Cartier eyewear features a precise palette of high-end materials: solid 18k gold and platinum on the premium tier, lightweight yet strong titanium, rich Italian acetate in tortoiseshell, honey, smoke, and saturated colors, plus select buffalo horn and exotic acetates on Première pieces.

Every frame is built to last decades. We take the time to explain the differences in weight, adjustability, and feel so you choose the right one for your lifestyle.

Ready for a proper Cartier fitting in Old Bridge, NJ? Our selection changes often — call ahead, tell us your preferred silhouette, and we will have pieces ready when you arrive.

What a Cartier fitting looks like

When you come in for a Cartier optical fitting, we take the piece through its proper paces. We try the silhouette against your proportion, compare it in different light, and study how it sits across the brow. The nose-pad, temple length, and pantoscopic tilt are all adjusted in-house. We will not rush you, and we will not pretend something is right when it is not. If a different Cartier frame — or a different designer — is the better answer, we will say so.

Prescription lenses, done properly

Cartier frames are fully prescription-ready. We work with single-vision, progressive, high-index, polarized, and specialty photochromic and blue-light lenses. Our lens counsel is plainspoken — we will explain the trade-offs, recommend what suits your prescription and your day, and leave the decision with you. For complex prescriptions, we take the time to measure properly and to explain what a given lens can and cannot do in a given frame.

Care that outlasts the season

Cartier eyewear is made to be worn for years. We treat it accordingly. Nose-pad replacements, hinge adjustments, temple-tip work, and screw replacements are part of the relationship — stop in for a quick adjustment whenever you need one. Frames you bought elsewhere are welcome as well; the care is the point.

Plan your visit

Tell us the silhouette you have in mind — rectangle, round, aviator, panthère-inspired — and whether you are buying optical, sun, or both. We will pull pieces in your range so your fitting is productive from the moment you sit down.
