Designer eyewear near Matawan, NJ.

Eye Atelier is minutes from Matawan — a luxury optical boutique carrying Cartier, Dior, Gucci, Fendi, Loewe, Dita, and Oliver Peoples.

Matawan is close. Most of our Matawan clients drive over to The Shoppes at Old Bridge in under ten minutes. The boutique sits just off Route 9, with ample parking steps from the door. If you have been looking for a designer eyewear boutique that feels like a destination rather than a kiosk, Eye Atelier is the short drive worth making.

Our optical and sun curation is organized around the couture houses — Cartier, Dior, Gucci, Fendi, Balmain, Loewe, Dita, Oliver Peoples, Maui Jim, Chanel — and around a simple proposition: a designer frame deserves a real fitting.

Why Matawan clients come here

Matawan clients usually arrive with one of two intentions: a specific designer piece in mind (often a Cartier), or the desire to be walked through a real curation and helped to make a considered choice. We do both. The selection is edited. The fitting is unhurried. Adjustments are done in-house. The counsel is plainspoken.

What an Eye Atelier fitting looks like

Plan on thirty to forty-five minutes. We start with proportion and with how you actually live in glasses. We pull pieces across silhouettes — not just across price — and adjust temple length, nose-pad angle, bridge fit, and tilt in-house. If a frame is close but not right, we keep going. If it is the wrong piece for you, we say so.

Lenses, done properly

Single vision, progressive, high-index, polarized, photochromic, specialty — cut in-house with proper measurements. For progressive wearers, we take pupillary distance, segment height, and fitting height carefully. For complex prescriptions, we will tell you before we cut what a given frame can and cannot gracefully carry.

Exams and fittings in one visit

If you are driving over from Matawan, consider pairing a comprehensive eye exam with your fitting. Call ahead and we will schedule them back-to-back so your drive is as efficient as the selection.
